  • PAINTINGS

    The expression of ideas and emotions, with the creation of certain aesthetic qualities, in a two-dimensional visual language.   • Travelling painting

    How do you travel and paint? Keep your paints in a separate plastic bag in case they leak during the flight and pack them in your suitcase (don't carry them on the plane). I suggest packing the paints in a clear plastic container. Be sure and enclose your paints with the Plein Air Magazine label.

  • About our artists

    Leonardo da Vinci

    Leonardo da Vinci (1452–1519) Regarded as one of the greatest artists of all time, he is well known for his two remarkable paintings: The Mona Lisa and The Last Supper

    Michelangelo

    Rembrandt (1606–1669) Known as the “Dutch Master”, he is considered one of the greatest visual artists in the history of art. His famous masterpieces include The Night Watch and The Return of the Prodigal Son.

    Pablo Picasso

    Pablo Picasso (1881-1973) A child prodigy, he finished his first painting, The Picador at just 9 years old! His most popular paintings were Guernica, Les Demoiselles d'Avignon and The Weeping Woman.
